  1. website
    1. our website's home page (https://big.dougie.tech) uses a 3rd party analytics provider, Google Analytics, which may set cookies on your device. we use our analytics provider to measure the number of page views on our website in aggregate and the sources from which our users find our website. google analytics or other analytics measures are not employed on any other pages of our website.
  2. slack app
    1. big text receives the following information about its users when they authorize us:
      1. access tokens, which allow us to post messages on your behalf
      2. the slack user ID and team ID - allows us to associate your use of our Slack commands to tokens
      3. slack display names and email addresses - provided automatically by Slack, not stored
      4. name of your slack team (e.g. Douglas Technologies) - provided automatically by Slack, not stored
    2. big text only stores Slack user IDs, team IDs and access tokens. we do not store your display name or email address. we additionally do not request permissions from Slack that would enable us to later request personal information about you. big text does not have read access to any Slack conversations, DMs, etc.
    3. information we collect is not shared with any 3rd parties under any circumstance.
    4. at your request, big text will delete the stored access tokens. such requests may be made by emailing privacy@dougie.tech you may also revoke your authorization directly through Slack, which renders the tokens we have invalid.
    5. because big text does not associate the creator of an image with the image itself, we cannot honor request to delete all images created by a particular user.
